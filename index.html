Questions & answers

A few things our guests often ask

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Both are welcome! Walk-ins are always accepted when we have an opening, but booking ahead guarantees your spot and your favorite nail tech.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash and all major credit cards.

Where do I park?

There's free parking right in front of the salon — we're located next to O'Reilly Auto Parts on FM1314.

How early should I arrive?

Arriving about 5 minutes early helps us start your appointment on time. If you're running late, give us a quick call.

How can I reach you?

The easiest way is to call us at (281) 747-7421. We're happy to answer any questions or help you book.
